Omlette

Its a classic breakfast dish that's super versatile.It's a quick and easy meal option,perfect for busy mornings or when you want a simple yet satisfying dish.

Preparation time

  • Total:Approximately 35 min
  • Preparation:Approximately 25 min
  • Cooking:Approximately 10 min

Ingredients

  • 3-4 medium size Potato
  • salt,to taste
  • Pepper,to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of oil
  • chilli powder, turmeric powder,curry leaves

Instructions

1.

Peel the potatoes and rinse them under cold water to remove any dirt. Pat them dry with a kitchen towel.

2.

Heat the panCut the potatoes into cubes or thin slices, depending on your preference. Make sure the pieces are roughly the same size so they cook evenly. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Once the oil is hot, add the potato cubes/slices to the pan.

4.

Fry the potatoes for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until they start to turn golden brown. Reduce the heat to low and add turmeric powder, red chili powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, and salt to the potatoes. Mix well to ensure the spices are evenly distributed.

5.

Continue to cook the potatoes on low heat for another 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until they are cooked through and crispy on the outside. You can cover the pan with a lid to help the potatoes cook faster, but remember to stir occasionally to prevent sticking. Once the potatoes are cooked to your desired level of crispiness, turn off the heat and transfer them to a serving dish.

6.

Enjoy:Garnish the potato fry with chopped coriander leaves, if desired, and serve hot as a side dish or snack. Enjoy your delicious homemade potato fry! .
