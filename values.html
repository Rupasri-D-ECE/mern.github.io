Paneer Tika Masala

Its a popular dish in Indian cuisine.Its usually served with naan bread or rice.Its a flavorful and satisfying dish thats loved by many!

Preparation time

  • Total:Approximately 10 min
  • Preparation:Approximately 5 min
  • Cooking:Approximately 5 min

Ingredients

  • 200 grams of paneer, cut into cubes
  • 1 bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 onion, cut into chunks
  • 2 tablespoons yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon chilli powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

1.

In a bowl, combine yogurt, tomato paste, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric powder, chili powder, garam masala, cumin powder, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well to make a marinade.

3.

Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment pape

4.

Arrange the marinated paneer, bell pe and onion on the baking sheet. Drizzle with spoon.

5.
